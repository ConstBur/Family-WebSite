Park Omega: The Miracles of Nature

Back in March, we decided to go to Omega Park with the whole family. We were going there for a long time, but somehow it did not work out. But this time, seemingly on a calendar spring day (in fact frosty and snowy), we went there...

The most interesting advantage of this park is that it is NOT forbidden to feed animals on its territory, but it is even welcomed. Before going on a trip, we carefully studied its website to be aware of the prices, the duration of the trip and the basic rules of conduct during the visit. Here the excursion in the animal world takes place without leaving your car. You can open windows by half. To go out of the car is prohibited .. And in the habitat of bears-grizzly windows, it is recommended to close them entirely. And... that's pretty much it!

The nature here is simply unusually picturesque! On such vast expanses, among the animals you really feel like their guest. In addition, the little animals behaved quite vividly, I would even say rather impudently. It does not take much effort to call them for carrots - they themselves know where to go to get enough. A real paradise for them!

It was a wonderful day! Sure, someday we will return to this wonderful place once again.
